  • Here are the rules for playing Rock-Paper-Scissors
  • Two players participate.
  • Gestures: Rock (fist), Paper (open hand), Scissors (V fingers).
  • Determine the winner: Rock crushes Scissors, Scissors cuts Paper, Paper covers Rock.
  • Replay if both players choose the same shape.
  • Say "Rock, Paper, Scissors, Shoot!" and show shapes on "Shoot."
